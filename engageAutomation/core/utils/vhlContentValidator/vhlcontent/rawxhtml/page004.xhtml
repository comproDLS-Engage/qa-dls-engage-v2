Build Background

WAYS TO TALK ABOUT NAMES

Read the paragraphs and answer the questions.

My name is Rosa Montoya. First name, Rosa; last name, Montoya. I have a middle name, but I don't use it. My full name is Rosa Carina Montoya. On official documents, like my birth certificate, that's my name. At school, I have a nickname: Roro. My friends call me Roro. At home, I have a different nickname: Rosita. My whole family calls me Rosita.

It's interesting to think about my first name. Sometimes I practice my signature, and sometimes I think about how I would change my name if I could.
